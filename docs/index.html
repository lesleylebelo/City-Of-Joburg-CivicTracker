About CivicTracker

CivicTrack is a web application built exclusively for residents of the City of Joburg municipality. The platform bridges the gap between residents and the city by providing a centralised space to:

.Report civic and municipal issues in an area
.Track ongoing maintenance work and new development projects across the region
.Stay informed on what's happening in your neighbourhood and beyond

CivicTrack is designed exclusively for City of Joburg residents. The system uses location-based filtering to verify that users and submitted reports fall within the official CoJ municipal boundary. Any activity detected outside this boundary is automatically restricted.

Use Cases

Report Civic Issues

Report problems affecting your community. Submit reports with descriptions, photos, and locations for faster municipal response.

Track Report Status

Monitor the progress of issues you have reported. Get full transparency from reporting to resolution.

Monitor Municipal Projects

Stay informed about projects happening in your area.Know what projects are underway, their progress, budgets, and expected completion dates.

Community Polls and Voting

Participate in polls and provide input on local matters. Get a voice in local decision-making and helps the municipality understand community priorities.

Community Events

Discover and stay informed about municipal and community events. Encourages citizen participation and strengthens community engagement.

Maintenance Tracking

Monitor the progress of repairs and maintenance work in your area in real time.

Municipal Projects Monitor

Real-Time Notifications

Receive updates on your reports, ongoing projects, and important municipal announcements.
