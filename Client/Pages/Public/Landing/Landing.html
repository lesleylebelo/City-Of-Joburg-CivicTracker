About CivicTracker

The CivicTracker project empowers Johannesburg residents to report issues, track progress, and stay informed about municipal services. Our goal is to create a transparent, interactive platform that connects citizens with local government efficiently.

Use Cases

New Building Projects

Access real-time updates on municipal projects and services.

New Building Projects

Construction of a modern commercial and residential tower in the heart of Johannesburg, combining sustainable design with advanced urban facilities.

Potholes Repair Initiative

Identifying and repairing potholes across Johannesburg to improve road safety, reduce vehicle damage, and ensure smoother traffic flow for residents and commuters.

Features

Issue Reporting

Quickly report potholes, broken streetlights, water leaks, and other service delivery issues.

Maintenance Tracking

Monitor the progress of repairs and maintenance work in your area in real time.

Development Project Tracking

Stay informed about new building developments and infrastructure projects happening across Johannesburg.

Real-Time Notifications

Receive updates on your reports, ongoing projects, and important municipal announcements.

Interactive Maps

View reported issues and development projects visually across the city using map-based tools.

User Dashboard

Access a personalized dashboard to track your reports, view updates, and manage your activity.
